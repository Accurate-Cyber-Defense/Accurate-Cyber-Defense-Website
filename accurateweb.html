About Accurate Cyber Defense

Accurate Cyber Defense is a Malawian cybersecurity startup established in 2022 to address the growing cybersecurity needs in Malawi and the surrounding region. As a homegrown company, we understand the unique digital challenges faced by local businesses and institutions.

Our team is made up of certified cybersecurity professionals with international experience who have returned to Malawi with a shared mission: to contribute to the country’s digital transformation while ensuring its safety and resilience.

We pride ourselves on delivering world-class cybersecurity solutions at competitive prices, making enterprise-grade protection accessible to businesses of all sizes across Malawi. Accurate Cyber Defense is committed to being at the forefront of cybersecurity innovation in the region.
